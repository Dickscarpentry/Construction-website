Carpentry services

Dick's Carpentry provides practical, high-quality timber work for residential and commercial jobs. Whether you need a single door hanging or a multi-skill property update, you’ll get clear communication and a finish you can be proud of.

  • Doors & frames — internal doors, entrance doors, ironmongery
  • Kitchens — fitting support and finishing joinery
  • Feature & media walls, panelling, stair rails
  • External carpentry — decking, pergolas, gates and fencing
  • 2nd fix and multi-skill refurbishments
